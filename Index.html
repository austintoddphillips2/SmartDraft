How to Play

Build the best basketball roster without breaking the salary cap.

1. Choose a run

Quick Run gives you a fresh random board. Daily Challenge changes automatically with the local calendar date and locks an identical 8-team, 7-player format for score chasing.

2. Build a story

Choose a franchise identity, capture round bounties, and build fan hype. Each identity rewards a different drafting style.

3. Manage your cap

Your possible round spending cannot exceed your remaining salary cap. Bids use $0.1M increments and must meet each player's minimum. You can cut a player during the draft and recover 70% of his contract.

4. Call Your Shot

Once per run, predict a player you will win. Nail it below market for a huge hype payoff; miss and the crowd turns.

5. Chase missions

Each run gives you three build goals and one rival GM. Missions add a small signature bonus to your final score.

6. Simulate the season

Choose an offense and defense, assign five starters to PG–C, then play the tournament. Your final card is built to screenshot and share.

🔥 Round Bounty

One player on every board is marked as the bounty. Win him to earn a large Fan Hype bonus. The bounty does not alter his rating, price, or CPU demand—it simply tempts you into making a louder decision.

🎯 Call Your Shot

Once per draft, mark a player and submit a real bid on him. Winning earns bonus Hype, with an extra reward for paying at or below value. Losing costs Hype and uses the prediction permanently.

📈 Fan Hype

Hype measures the story of your run: stars, steals, bounties, missions, rival wins, and championships raise it. Reckless overpays and missed predictions can lower it. Up to five final-score points come from Hype.

🧬 Team Identity

Your personnel creates a basketball identity such as Five-Out Machine, Paint Punishers, Transition Terror, or Defensive Pressure. The right gameplan amplifies it during simulation—even imperfect teams can find a winning style.

Quick advice: Stars appear more often early, but value exists throughout the draft. Spending everything immediately can leave weak auto-filled roster spots; waiting too long can leave you without elite talent.
